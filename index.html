Tanjiro Kamado

Kind Brave Strong

Summary

Tanjiro is the main protagonist of the anime and manga series, Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. After his father Tanjuro Kamado passed away due to illness, his mother was left with 6 children. As the oldest son Tanjiro had the resposibility of taking care of his younger siblings and his mother.

Fun Facts

  • He has a really hard forehead, which came from his mothers side.
  • He can understand what birds are saying.
  • He is a very light sleeper.
  • He is a horrible singer.
  • He has an extraordinary sense of smell.
  • He can smell if someone near is on the verge of death.
  • He is great judge of character.
  • His family used to deal in charcoal, and the first kanji for his name, "tan", is charcoal in Japanese.
  • He is a terrible liar.
  • He got the scar on his head from protecting his younger brother from a falling pot of boiling tea.
